DreamTeam.gg



What is this thing?

DreamTeam.gg is a site where you can enter 5 summoner names, and it generates the best possible team, based on Mastery Points. It puts each person in their strongest position, with their best champion in that position. If there are two people who are both top tier junglers, but one of them is garbage everywhere else and the other is pretty good in Mid, then it will put the first person in jungle and the second in mid. The goal of this site is to do all the hard thinking about best possible combinations so you and your team don't have to.
Let us know what you think about it!

Developed by KmancXC and Jiaxsun
